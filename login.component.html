FAQ

Got questions?

Everything you need to know about Cryptara.

💰 Is Cryptara free to use?

Yes, completely free. You get a $10,000 virtual balance to start paper trading immediately — no credit card required.

📡 Where does market data come from?

All price data is streamed live from Binance via WebSocket, giving you real-time order books, trades and ticker prices.

🔒 Is my account data secure?

Passwords are hashed with bcrypt, sessions managed with JWT + Redis, and all data is encrypted in transit.

📊 What can I trade on Cryptara?

Currently BTC, ETH, SOL, BNB, XRP and ADA — all paired with USDT. More pairs coming soon.

⚡ What order types are supported?

Market orders (instant fill) and limit orders (fill when price reaches your target) are both supported.

🎯 Can I reset my portfolio?

Yes — reset your virtual balance and portfolio at any time from account settings to start fresh.
